# MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

**THIS NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT** (the "Agreement") is entered into on this 15th day of April, 2026 (the "Effective Date"),

**BETWEEN:**

**TECHCORP INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED**, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at Plot No. 42, Bandra Kurla Complex, Mumbai – 400051, Maharashtra, India (hereinafter referred to as the **"Disclosing Party"** or **"TechCorp"**, which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context, include its successors and permitted assigns);

**AND**

**STARTUP SOLUTIONS LLP**, a limited liability partnership registered under the Limited Liability Partnership Act, 2008, having its registered office at 3rd Floor, Prestige Towers, Koramangala, Bengaluru – 560034, Karnataka, India (hereinafter referred to as the **"Receiving Party"** or **"Startup Solutions"**, which expression shall, unless repugnant to the context, include its successors and permitted assigns).

TechCorp and Startup Solutions are hereinafter collectively referred to as the **"Parties"** and individually as a **"Party"**.

## RECITALS

**WHEREAS**, the Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship involving the development of a cloud-based SaaS platform (the "Purpose");

**WHEREAS**, in connection with the Purpose, each Party may disclose certain proprietary and confidential information to the other;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the Parties agree as follows:

## 1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" shall mean **any and all information** disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, in any form whatsoever, whether oral, written, electronic, visual, or any other form, whether or not marked as confidential, including but not limited to all business information, technical data, trade secrets, know-how, customer lists, financial information, marketing plans, employee information, source code, algorithms, designs, drawings, specifications, **any information the Receiving Party may learn, observe, or deduce from any interaction with the Disclosing Party or any of its affiliates, employees, agents, vendors, or customers, whether or not such information is expressly designated as confidential**, and any and all derivatives, compilations, or analyses thereof, in perpetuity.

## 2. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

The Receiving Party covenants and agrees that for a period of **three (3) years following the termination or expiration of this Agreement**, it shall not, directly or indirectly, whether as principal, agent, employee, consultant, shareholder, or otherwise:

(a) engage in, carry on, or be interested in any business that competes with the Disclosing Party anywhere in India or globally;

(b) solicit, entice, or attempt to solicit any employee, customer, vendor, or business partner of the Disclosing Party;

(c) develop, market, or sell any product or service that is similar to, or competitive with, any product or service of the Disclosing Party.

## 3. INDEMNIFICATION

The **Receiving Party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless** the Disclosing Party, its directors, officers, employees, affiliates, agents, and representatives from and against **any and all claims, losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including attorneys' fees on a full indemnity basis)** arising out of or in connection with (i) any breach or alleged breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party; (ii) any unauthorized use or disclosure of Confidential Information; (iii) any claim by a third party relating to the Receiving Party's use of Confidential Information; and (iv) any other act or omission of the Receiving Party, **regardless of whether such claim, loss, or damage was foreseeable, and without any cap or limitation on liability**. The Disclosing Party shall have no reciprocal indemnification obligation under this Agreement.

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY OWNERSHIP

All intellectual property rights, including but not limited to patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and any other proprietary rights, in **any work product, ideas, improvements, inventions, discoveries, concepts, or developments** conceived, developed, or reduced to practice by the Receiving Party, its employees, contractors, or agents **during the term of this Agreement or within two (2) years thereafter, whether or not related to the Purpose of this Agreement, whether developed on the Receiving Party's own time or using the Receiving Party's own resources**, shall automatically and irrevocably vest in and be the exclusive property of the Disclosing Party. The Receiving Party shall execute all documents necessary to perfect such ownership.

## 5. DATA PRIVACY AND PROTECTION

To the extent that any Confidential Information includes "sensitive personal data or information" within the meaning of the **Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011**, the Receiving Party agrees to:

(a) implement reasonable security practices and procedures as required under **Section 43A of the IT Act, 2000**;

(b) use such data only for the Purpose of this Agreement;

(c) notify the Disclosing Party promptly of any data breach or unauthorized access;

(d) comply with all applicable data protection laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, as and when notified.

## 6. TERM AND TERMINATION

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of **two (2) years**, unless earlier terminated by either Party with **thirty (30) days' written notice**. Notwithstanding termination, the confidentiality obligations under Clause 1 shall survive for a period of five (5) years from the date of termination.

## 7. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the **laws of India**. The Parties agree that any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be subject to the **exclusive jurisdiction of the courts at Mumbai, Maharashtra**.

## 8. NOTICES

All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by hand, courier, registered post, or email to the addresses set forth above.

## 9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, whether written or oral.
